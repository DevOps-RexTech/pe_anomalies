3 Usage Modes · Decision Flow · MTTR Impact

Mode 01 · Early Warning
Detection Before Break
Catch it at ingestion, not at EOD
  • 1Vendor feed arrives in S3
  • 2Lambda validates schema, timestamps, completeness
  • 3Anomaly flagged — missing securities, late arrival, price spike
  • 4Slack alert fires to ops team immediately
  • 5Ops chases vendor OR uses fallback data with flag
✓ Problem resolved before reconciliation even runs — no break at all
Mode 02 · Break Analysis
Root Cause in 5 Minutes
Known pattern → instant resolution
  • 1Reconciliation break detected at EOD
  • 2RAG engine searches historical incident database
  • 3Finds matching pattern from Oct 2023 — Bloomberg corp action lag
  • 4Bedrock generates: root cause + exact resolution steps
  • 5Junior ops applies fix — no senior escalation needed
✓ MTTR drops from 45 mins to under 5 mins for known patterns
Mode 03 · Smart Escalation
Unknown Break Triage
New pattern → escalate with full context
  • 1Break detected — RAG finds no strong historical match
  • 2Bedrock still generates best-effort analysis with caveats
  • 3PagerDuty fires to senior ops with full AI summary attached
  • 4Senior staff arrive with context — no 20-min briefing needed
  • 5Resolution stored → next time it becomes Mode 02
✓ Escalation still happens but senior staff are pre-briefed — saves 20 mins
